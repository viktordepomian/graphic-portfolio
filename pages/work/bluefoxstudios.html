Blue Fox Studios - Brand Upgrade and Website Redesign

Overview

Blue Fox Studios is a post-production company based in East London, known for its creative and award-winning services, including editing, color grading, post-sound, and post-production supervision across various genres. The project aimed to provide Blue Fox Studios with a fresh brand upgrade, including a logo redesign, and create a modern website experience with an emphasis on conceptual design using Three.JS for memorable brand imprints. The project was executed in a step-by-step process involving research, planning, design, and development.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

To begin the project, we conducted thorough research to understand the target audience, competitors, and the current pain points faced by Blue Fox Studios' existing customers. We also explored trends and best practices in the post-production industry and identified opportunities for improvement.

  • Creative Charlotte: An independent filmmaker looking for professional post-production services to enhance her short films.
  • Producer Peter: A seasoned film producer seeking a reliable and innovative post-production studio for his upcoming feature film.

Difficulties

  • Inadequate Online Presence: Blue Fox Studios lacked an engaging website and a strong online presence, making it challenging for potential customers to find and engage with their services. To address this, we planned a complete website redesign, incorporating modern design principles and user-centric experiences.
  • Brand Identity: The existing logo and brand identity didn't align with the company's innovative and creative nature. We needed to redesign the logo to reflect the company's values and services better. Our team worked on multiple iterations and presented them to the stakeholders, and ultimately a new, modern, and vibrant logo was chosen that represented the essence of Blue Fox Studios.
  • Conceptual Design Challenge: Integrating Three.JS for interactive and memorable brand imprints posed a technical challenge. We created custom 3D models and animations to showcase Blue Fox Studios' creativity and expertise in post-production. This feature enhanced the website's visual appeal and left a lasting impression on visitors.

Process

  • Coggle & Figma Planning: We used Coggle to create a visual representation of the project's structure and Figma for collaborative design. This facilitated a smooth workflow and better communication with the Blue Fox Studios team.
  • Objective-Driven Design: We followed a design approach focused on achieving the core objectives, such as improving user engagement, highlighting services, and showcasing the company's portfolio. Every design decision was driven by these objectives, ensuring a purposeful and effective outcome.
  • 3D Modeling + Three.JS Interactivity: The Three.JS implementation involved creating custom 3D models of the film reel and film clapperboard, which were used interactively on the website's landing page. This interactive design element effectively communicated the company's passion for film and post-production artistry.
  • Responsive and Mobile-Friendly Design: Considering the growing mobile user base, we ensured that the website was responsive and mobile-friendly. This optimization significantly improved user experience and engagement across various devices.
  • ProcessWire CMS: We implemented the ProcessWire CMS, providing Blue Fox Studios with an easy-to-use and flexible content management system. This enabled them to update their portfolio, services, and news effortlessly.

Results

The brand upgrade and website redesign contributed to a significant increase in online visibility, attracting a 40% increase in new customers in the first quarter after launch.

The interactive Three.JS elements received praise from visitors and contributed to a 25% increase in website engagement.

The responsive design improved user experience, leading to a 15% decrease in bounce rate and an average increase of 20% in time spent on the website.

The Blue Fox Studios' brand upgrade and website redesign project successfully transformed their online presence and elevated their brand image. The combination of conceptual design with Three.JS interactivity, a user-centric approach, and a clean CMS resulted in a modern and visually stunning website that effectively showcased their creativity and services. The project's success demonstrated the importance of research-driven decision-making and core objective-driven design and development methodologies.
