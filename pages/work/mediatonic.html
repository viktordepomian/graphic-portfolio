MediaTonic - A Seamless Gaming Platform for All

Overview

Mediatonic, a renowned game development company with a rich portfolio of successful games, has decided to expand its reach by creating a new gaming platform called "MediaTonic Arcade." The goal is to provide a seamless and engaging gaming experience to players across different demographics and interests. As a UX designer at Mediatonic, I was entrusted with the task of creating an intuitive and user-friendly interface for the platform.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

To kickstart the project, I conducted extensive market research to understand the preferences and pain points of potential users. I also collaborated with the marketing team to gather insights from existing Mediatonic game players. Based on this research, I created three primary personas:

  • Casual Gamer Cathy: A 30-year-old working professional who enjoys playing games in her leisure time to relax and de-stress.
  • Social Gamer Sam: A 24-year-old college student who loves competitive multiplayer games and enjoys engaging with friends while playing.
  • Strategy Gamer Steve: A 42-year-old tech-savvy gamer who prefers challenging strategy-based games and often spends considerable time on gaming platforms.

Challenges

  • Diverse User Base: Catering to the preferences of different personas while maintaining a cohesive and engaging user experience was a major challenge.
  • Brand Consistency: Ensuring that the platform's design aligns with Mediatonic's brand identity while still giving MediaTonic Arcade its distinct personality was vital.
  • Onboarding Complexity: With a diverse game library, guiding new users through the onboarding process was complex, as we needed to strike a balance between simplicity and showcasing the platform's features.

Solutions Implemented

  • Persona-Centric Design: I crafted separate user journeys for each persona, tailoring the platform's UI and interactions to meet their specific needs and expectations. This approach significantly improved user satisfaction and retention.
  • Design System Implementation: To address the brand consistency challenge, I established a comprehensive design system that included color schemes, typography, and UI components. This ensured that the platform's interface felt unified while allowing room for creativity in individual game designs.
  • Gamified Onboarding: To make onboarding engaging and intuitive, I designed a gamified onboarding process where users could explore different platform features by completing quests and earning rewards. This approach increased user retention during the onboarding phase.

Results and Impact

The gamified onboarding process contributed to a 25% decrease in drop-off rates during the first week of user sign-ups. MediaTonic received overwhelmingly positive feedback from users, with an average satisfaction rating of 4.8/5.

By applying persona-centric design, implementing a cohesive design system, and creating a gamified onboarding experience, MediaTonic Arcade has become a thriving gaming platform loved by players of all types. The project was a testament to the power of user-centric design and showcased Mediatonic's commitment to delivering high-quality gaming experiences to its audience.
