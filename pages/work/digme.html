Digme - Elevating Fitness Experience

Overview

Digme is a cutting-edge fitness studio that offers inspiring and energetic cycling and HiiT classes, elevating fitness into a lifestyle journey. To enhance customer experience and boost business growth, Digme sought to revamp their online presence, including a bespoke booking and payment engine, as well as developing mobile apps for iOS and Android. This UX project aimed to create a seamless, engaging, and user-friendly digital ecosystem that fosters customer loyalty and attracts new users.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

The project began with comprehensive research, including interviews, surveys, and competitor analysis. Three primary personas were identified:

  • Energetic Emma: A 28-year-old marketing executive seeking a fun and challenging fitness routine to balance her fast-paced life.
  • Healthy Harry: A 35-year-old fitness enthusiast who values convenience and is keen on tracking his progress through the app.
  • Newbie Nicole: A 40-year-old individual new to fitness, looking for a supportive and welcoming environment.

Challenges

  • Creating a cohesive brand experience: Integrating the high-energy studio atmosphere into the digital experience was challenging.
  • Gamification without distraction: Implementing gamification in the app to drive engagement without compromising the core fitness experience.
  • Seamless booking and payment process: Developing a bespoke booking and payment engine that offers a smooth and efficient user experience.

Solutions Implemented

  • Brand exploration: Leveraging bright and dynamic color schemes, inspirational visuals, and motivational messaging to echo the studio experience.
  • Objective-driven design: Prioritizing the main objectives of each user persona to create an intuitive and focused design throughout the platform.
  • Responsive, mobile, and tablet friendly: Designing a fully responsive website and apps to ensure seamless access and engagement across various devices.
  • Objective-driven development: Utilizing an agile development approach to align development with the core objectives, ensuring faster implementation and iteration.
  • Multiple API integrations: Integrating fitness trackers, social media sharing, and rewards systems to enhance user engagement.
  • Digme API and booking engine: Developing a robust API and a bespoke booking engine for hassle-free class booking and secure payments.
  • Modular CMS: Employing a modular content management framework to enable easy content updates and scalability.
  • App gamification: Implementing gamified elements like point systems, achievement badges, and challenges, promoting fun and healthy competition.

Results

After the successful launch of the revamped digital ecosystem, Digme witnessed significant improvements. Digme experienced a 23% increase in customer retention, thanks too the engaging app experience and the new fitness tracking. It also saw an increase in new customers, as much as an 18% increase within the first three months of the launch, mainly by word-of-mouth referrals.

The app itself saw an increase of 35% in engagement. Thanks too the gamification, as users eagerly completed challenges or earned rewards.

The booking process was also made shorter which reduced the average booking time by 40%.

In conclusion, the Digme UX project successfully transformed the fitness studio's digital presence into an engaging and user-centric platform. By understanding user needs and crafting a cohesive brand experience, Digme elevated its fitness journey beyond the studio walls, fostering customer loyalty and attracting a growing user base.
