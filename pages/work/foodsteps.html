Crown Agents Bank Website Modernization

Overview

Foodsteps is an innovative platform designed to measure and reduce the environmental footprint of food from its production to consumption. The core objective of the project was to create an all-in-one platform that empowers individuals and businesses to make informed choices about their food consumption and its impact on the environment.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

To kickstart the project, extensive user research was conducted, including surveys, interviews, and market analysis. The research identified two primary user personas:

  • Eco-conscious Consumers (Sarah) Sarah is a 30-year-old environmental enthusiast who is passionate about sustainability and reducing her ecological impact. She regularly shops for organic and ethically sourced food, but she lacks an easy way to track the carbon footprint of her purchases.
  • Sustainable Restaurants (David): David is a 40-year-old restaurant owner who believes in offering eco-friendly dining options to his customers. He seeks a reliable platform to track the environmental impact of his restaurant's menu items and make data-driven decisions to reduce its carbon footprint.

Difficulties and Solutions:

  • Data Integration and Accuracy: One of the significant challenges was sourcing accurate and up-to-date data on the carbon footprint of various food products. Different sources provided conflicting information, requiring a careful selection process to ensure reliable data representation. To tackle data accuracy challenges, we collaborated with reputable environmental organizations and farms to obtain reliable data. We also allowed users to suggest updates, fostering a collaborative community for data verification.
  • User Interface Complexity: Creating a user-friendly interface that effectively communicated the complex data on carbon footprints and impact hotspots was demanding. We had to strike a balance between presenting detailed information and maintaining an intuitive user experience for both Sarah and David. We focused on Sarah and David's pain points and expectations while designing the interface. User testing and feedback were continuously integrated to refine the design and ensure an enjoyable experience for all users.
  • Performance Optimization With a vast amount of data to process and present, optimizing platform performance on different devices was crucial. Ensuring smooth user interactions across various screen sizes and internet speeds required rigorous testing and optimization. We opted for a PWA approach to optimize performance and enable offline access. This decision significantly improved platform accessibility, especially for users with limited internet connectivity.

Results and Impact:

Within six months of its launch, Foodsteps gained significant traction in the market. The platform saw a 42% increase in new users, with Sarah and David representing the majority of the user base. Eco-conscious consumers praised the platform's ease of use and how it helped them make sustainable food choices.

Foodsteps also formed partnerships with several renowned sustainability-driven restaurants, resulting in a 37% reduction in their overall carbon footprint within the first year of implementation. The platform's comprehensive reports and carbon footprint labels empowered restaurants like David's to showcase their efforts towards sustainability, attracting environmentally conscious customers.

Foodsteps successfully delivered an all-in-one solution for measuring and reducing the environmental footprint of food. Through data collaboration, user-centric design, and PWA development, the platform achieved substantial user adoption and contributed to reducing the carbon footprint in the food industry. As the project continues to grow, Foodsteps aims to expand its partnerships with sustainable farms and restaurants worldwide, making a significant positive impact on our planet's environmental health.
