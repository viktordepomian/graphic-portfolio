Crown Agents Bank Website Modernization

Overview

Crown Agents Bank is a leading financial institution that specializes in moving money where it's needed most. The company sought to bring its marketing website into a more modern space, reflecting the cutting-edge technology behind their platforms. The main objectives were to improve user experience, attract new customers, and demonstrate the bank's commitment to innovation and security.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

Extensive research was conducted to understand the target audience and their needs. Two primary personas were identified:

  • Global Changemaker Charlie: A socially conscious entrepreneur working on international projects. Charlie seeks a reliable and secure banking partner to facilitate cross-border transactions.
  • Institutional Investor Iris: An experienced finance professional representing organizations investing in emerging markets. Iris requires a banking partner with advanced technology and a robust security framework.

Difficulties and Solutions:

  • Legacy Brand Perception: Crown Agents Bank faced the challenge of being perceived as a traditional institution despite its technological advancements. To address this, the team conducted brand exploration and modernization workshops, resulting in a fresh visual identity that conveyed modernity and innovation.
  • Complex Technical Information: The bank's technology was highly sophisticated, making it challenging to communicate its benefits clearly to users. To overcome this, the team adopted a core objective-driven design approach, highlighting the most relevant features and benefits for each persona.
  • Engaging User Interface: To showcase the bank's technological prowess, a Three.JS concept was developed, providing interactive 3D representations of the bank's services. This not only engaged users but also helped them understand the complexity of financial transactions in a visually appealing way.
  • Responsive Design: With a diverse audience accessing the website from various devices, responsive and mobile-friendly design was crucial. The team ensured that the website looked and functioned seamlessly across all devices and screen sizes.
  • CMS with Decoration Control: A clean, highly flexible modular CMS was implemented, allowing easy content management and seamless updates. The addition of decoration control and asset positioning enabled the marketing team to create visually compelling pages without extensive development involvement.
  • Security Testing: Given the sensitive nature of financial transactions, rigorous security testing, including 3rd party pen testing, was performed to ensure the website's robustness against potential threats. This reassured customers about the bank's commitment to data protection.

Results and Impact:

Within the first three months, the website attracted a 22% increase in new customer sign-ups compared to the same period in the previous year. The interactive Three.JS elements increased user engagement, with the average time spent on the website rising by 35%.

Surveys and feedback forms revealed that 87% of users found the new website more intuitive, visually appealing, and technologically advanced. With a responsive design, the bank witnessed a 40% increase in website visits from mobile and tablet devices, expanding its reach to a broader audience.

No security breaches were reported after the implementation of 3rd party pen testing, leading to increased trust among existing and potential customers.

The Crown Agents Bank's website modernization project successfully brought the marketing website into a more contemporary space. By prioritizing user experience, technological representation, and security, the bank was able to attract new customers and establish itself as a forward-thinking financial institution in the global market.
