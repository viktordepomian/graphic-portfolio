Purfectly Fresh - Personalized Cat Food UX Redesign

Overview:

Purfectly Fresh is a UK-based cat food company that offers personalized, fresh, and real meat-based cat food tailored to each cat's unique nutritional and biological needs. The company's mission is to provide healthy and wholesome food for cats, challenging the existing commercial cat food brands that often prioritize profits over feline well-being. This project aimed to revamp Purfectly Fresh's website and user experience to improve customer engagement, increase new customer acquisition, and enhance user satisfaction.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Research and Persona Creation

To start the project, extensive research was conducted to understand the target audience, competitors, and industry best practices. The team also collected data on customer preferences, pain points, and their experiences with existing cat food brands.

  • Cat Lover Chris: A 30-year-old cat owner who is concerned about the quality of cat food in the market. Chris is health-conscious and desires personalized, nutritious options for their cat, Whiskers.
  • Busy Bee Bella: A 45-year-old working professional who owns three cats. Bella values convenience and time-efficiency, seeking a subscription-based cat food service that delivers personalized meals to her doorstep.

Difficulties & Solutions:

  • Designing a platform that efficiently generates personalized cat food plans based on each cat's unique needs was challenging. Solution: The team implemented an AI-driven questionnaire that users would fill out, providing essential details about their cat's age, breed, weight, activity level, and health conditions. The platform then utilized this data to generate a tailored meal plan for each cat.
  • Illustrating the concept of personalized cat food plans in an engaging and understandable way was difficult. Solution: The team created a set of playful illustrations that represented various cat breeds and health attributes. These illustrations were integrated into the AI-driven questionnaire and meal plan sections to visually communicate the personalization process.
  • Ensuring a seamless experience across different devices and screen sizes was a crucial aspect of the project. Solution: The team used Figma to plan and prototype the responsive design, meticulously testing and optimizing the user flow for desktop, mobile, and tablet users. The website was built using a mobile-first approach to guarantee a smooth experience on smaller screens.

Results

After the website redesign, Purfectly Fresh experienced a 40% increase in new customer sign-ups in the first two months. The Woocommerce + Woosubscription integration enabled easy subscription management, leading to a 30% increase in repeat customers.

Customer feedback showed a significant improvement in user satisfaction, with 92% of users expressing high levels of contentment with the personalized meal plans and overall website experience.

The UX redesign project for Purfectly Fresh successfully improved user engagement, customer acquisition, and satisfaction. By focusing on personalized experiences, playful illustrations, and responsive design, the company was able to provide fresh and real food that truly resonated with cat owners seeking better nutrition for their beloved feline companions.
