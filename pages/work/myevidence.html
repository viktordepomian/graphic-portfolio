My Evidence Medical Research Portal

Overview

My Evidence is a pioneering company in the medical industry that aims to produce and present detailed medical research. As part of their expansion, they sought to create an advanced Medical Research Portal that would provide healthcare professionals with easy access to evidence-based solutions. The goal was to streamline the research process, improve collaboration among medical practitioners, and increase the dissemination of critical medical findings.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Persona Creation

  • Dr. Emily Williams: A seasoned physician specializing in cardiology, eager to access the latest research and evidence-based treatment options to enhance patient care.
  • Nurse John Mitchell: A busy ER nurse looking for concise summaries of medical studies to stay updated with the latest practices and protocols.
  • Dr. Sarah Parker: A medical researcher striving to publish her work on the platform, seeking user-friendly tools to manage and present her research.

Difficulties & Solutions

  • Information Overload: With the abundance of medical data available, users were often overwhelmed with vast amounts of information. To address this, I implemented an intelligent search algorithm with filters and tags, allowing users to find relevant studies quickly.
  • Complex Navigation: Medical professionals require an intuitive platform. I employed core objective-driven design principles, where every action and page served a clear purpose. This streamlined navigation and made the platform easy to use.
  • Mobile Experience: Ensuring a seamless experience across devices was crucial. I employed a responsive design approach, allowing users to access the portal on their tablets or mobile phones while maintaining functionality and aesthetics.

Project Outcomes

The platform saw a significant increase in user engagement, with a 30% increase in returning visitors and a 20% decrease in bounce rates. Within the first six months, the portal attracted more than 10,000 new registered users, including medical professionals, researchers, and students.

The modular content management framework encouraged researchers to contribute their studies, leading to a 25% increase in research submissions.

User feedback has been overwhelmingly positive, with 90% of users reporting high satisfaction with the platform's ease of use and accessibility.

My Evidence's Medical Research Portal has become a game-changer in the medical industry, providing medical professionals with a reliable resource for evidence-based research. The project's success was a result of thorough research, user-centric design, and a focus on meeting the specific needs of the target audience. With ongoing updates and improvements, My Evidence continues to be at the forefront of advancing medical research and healthcare practices.
