Yiannimize Competitions Redesign

Overview:

Yiannimize Competitions is an extension of the renowned Yiannimize brand, offering users a chance to win exclusive prizes they could only dream of. The existing website faced usability and performance issues, resulting in a subpar user experience. The objective of this project was to redesign the website with a focus on enhancing user experience, improving performance, and increasing customer engagement.(Not all info or images are shown here, per the clients wishes)

Persona Creation:

  • Luxury Enthusiast Lucy is a 30-year-old marketing manager that aspire to win unique and high-end prizes. Hobbies are luxury cars, fashion and exclusive experiences.
  • Dreamer David is a 25-year-old college student that dreams of owning luxury items, but can't afford them. Hobbies are card, gaming and following different influencers.

Challenges

  • Engaging User Experience: The previous website lacked an immersive user experience, leading to a low percentage of visitors converting into participants.
  • Scalability & Performance: The website experienced performance issues during peak traffic, causing slow loading times and potential crashes.
  • Security & Data Protection: The platform required robust security measures to protect sensitive user data and transactions.

Solutions Implemented

  • Brand Exploration: Through iterative design processes, the website was designed with a clear focus on the Yiannimize brand while prioritizing the core objective of driving user engagement and participation.
  • Responsive Web Design (RWD): The new website was optimized for a seamless user experience across all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • Business Platform & Payment Gateway Integration: A custom business platform was developed to manage competitions, winners, and prizes effectively. Secure payment gateways and subscription integration were implemented to facilitate seamless transactions.
  • Modular CMS A flexible and modular CMS was integrated, enabling easy content management and updates. Additionally, a comprehensive reporting system was implemented to gather crucial insights and steer business decisions.
  • Clever Multi-Platform Interactive and engaging gameplay mechanics were incorporated into the website to encourage user participation and repeat visits.
  • Auto-Scaling AWS & Security Measures: AWS with auto-scaling capabilities was set up to handle high traffic volumes during peak times. Robust security measures, following OWASP Top 10 principles, were implemented to protect user data and maintain data integrity.

Results

The redesigned Yiannimize Competitions website achieved significant success within three months of its launch:

It witnessed a 45% increase in user engagement, with an average user spending 20% more time on the platform and a conversion rate of visitors to participants improved by 30%, with a notable increase in sign-ups.

The number of new customers increased by 55% in the first quarter, demonstrating a positive impact on the brand's reach and visibility.

The website remained stable and responsive, even during peak traffic times, leading to a 70% reduction in bounce rates.

In conclusion, the project successfully addressed the challenges and achieved substantial improvements in user experience, performance, and business outcomes. The website's enhanced capabilities and engaging features contributed significantly to Yiannimize Competitions' success in offering exclusive prizes and attracting a broader audience.
